About Hand2Hand

Empowering Education Through Inclusive Support

Who We Are

Hand2Hand is a revolutionary web-based scribe support system designed to promote inclusive and accessible examinations for students with disabilities. We believe that every student deserves equal opportunities to demonstrate their knowledge and abilities, regardless of physical limitations.

Our platform serves as a bridge between students who require scribe assistance and dedicated volunteers who are ready to support inclusive education. Through technology and compassion, we're making examination processes more accessible, efficient, and dignified.

The Problem We Solve

Many students with disabilities face significant challenges when it comes to examinations. Finding reliable and qualified scribes can be difficult, time-consuming, and stressful. Traditional methods of arranging scribe support often involve:

Manual coordination processes that are inefficient and prone to errors, lack of transparency in scribe allocation, difficulty in verifying scribe qualifications and availability, limited options and last-minute cancellations, and administrative burden on educational institutions.

Hand2Hand addresses all these challenges through a centralized, transparent, and user-friendly digital platform.
