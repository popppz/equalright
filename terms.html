2. User Responsibilities

2.1 For Students

  • Provide accurate and truthful information during registration
  • Submit valid documentation proving eligibility for scribe support
  • Inform the platform about any changes to examination schedules in advance
  • Treat assigned scribes with respect and professionalism
  • Notify administrators immediately if unable to attend a scheduled examination
  • Use the platform solely for legitimate academic examination support

2.2 For Scribes

  • Provide accurate qualifications and availability information
  • Maintain confidentiality of all examination content and student information
  • Arrive punctually for all assigned examination sessions
  • Write exactly what the student dictates without adding personal interpretations
  • Maintain neutrality and avoid influencing student responses
  • Report any issues or concerns to administrators promptly

2.3 For Administrators

  • Verify credentials of all registered students and scribes
  • Match students with appropriate scribes fairly and efficiently
  • Maintain confidentiality of all user information
  • Address complaints and issues in a timely manner
  • Ensure platform security and data protection

3. Code of Conduct

All users of the Hand2Hand platform must adhere to the following code of conduct:

  • Respect: Treat all users with dignity, courtesy, and professionalism
  • Honesty: Provide truthful information and maintain academic integrity
  • Confidentiality: Protect sensitive information shared through the platform
  • Punctuality: Honor scheduled commitments and time agreements
  • Non-discrimination: Do not discriminate based on disability, gender, religion, or any other factor
  • Appropriate Use: Use the platform only for its intended purpose of supporting inclusive examinations

4. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on the Hand2Hand platform:

  • Providing false or misleading information during registration
  • Using the platform for any form of academic dishonesty or cheating
  • Sharing login credentials with unauthorized persons
  • Attempting to access, modify, or damage platform data or systems
  • Harassing, threatening, or abusing other users
  • Misusing personal information obtained through the platform
