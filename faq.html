Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Hand2Hand

üìå General Questions

‚ùì What is Hand2Hand?
Hand2Hand is a web-based scribe support system designed to connect students with disabilities to qualified volunteer scribes for examinations. The platform streamlines the process of requesting, assigning, and managing scribe support in an efficient and transparent manner.
‚ùì Who can use Hand2Hand?
Hand2Hand is available to three types of users: (1) Students with disabilities who require scribe assistance during examinations, (2) Volunteers who want to serve as scribes, and (3) Educational institution administrators who manage and coordinate scribe assignments.
‚ùì Is Hand2Hand free to use?
Yes, Hand2Hand is completely free for both students and scribes. The platform is built on the principle of volunteer support and inclusive education. There are no charges for registration or using any features of the platform.
‚ùì How does Hand2Hand ensure quality?
Hand2Hand maintains quality through several mechanisms: verification of scribe qualifications by administrators, a rating and feedback system after each examination, background checks and credential verification, and a transparent system where students can view scribe ratings before assignments.

‚ùì How do I register as a student?
Click on "Student Register" in the navigation menu. Fill out the registration form with your personal details, disability information, and examination requirements. Upload your disability certificate (PDF, JPG, or PNG format). Submit the form and wait for admin approval. You'll receive a confirmation email once approved.
‚ùì What documents do I need to provide?
You need to provide a valid disability certificate issued by a medical professional or relevant authority. The certificate should clearly state your disability type and any specific requirements. Additional documents like student ID or institutional verification may be requested by administrators.
‚ùì How long does it take to get a scribe assigned?
After your registration is approved, scribe assignment typically takes 2-5 business days depending on availability and your examination schedule. We recommend registering at least 2 weeks before your examination date to ensure adequate time for matching and coordination.
‚ùì Can I request a specific scribe?
While you cannot directly request a specific scribe, you can specify preferences such as subject expertise, language preference, and availability. Our matching system and administrators consider these preferences when making assignments. If you've worked with a scribe before and had a positive experience, you can mention this in special requirements.
‚ùì What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
If you need to cancel or reschedule, contact us immediately through the Contact Us page or email support@hand2hand.edu. Please provide at least 48 hours notice when possible. This allows us to notify the scribe and potentially reassign them or find a replacement for your new date.
‚ùì Can I use Hand2Hand for multiple examinations?
Yes! Once you're registered, you can request scribe support for as many examinations as you need throughout your academic career. Simply log in to your dashboard and submit new requests for each examination.

‚úçÔ∏è For Scribes

‚ùì What qualifications do I need to become a scribe?
Scribes should have good writing skills, patience, and the ability to accurately transcribe spoken words. While formal qualifications aren't strictly required, we prefer volunteers with educational backgrounds relevant to the subjects they'll assist with. Most importantly, you must be able to write clearly, follow instructions, and maintain professionalism.
‚ùì How much time commitment is required?
The time commitment varies based on your availability and the number of assignments you accept. Each examination typically lasts 2-4 hours. You have full control over your availability schedule and can update it anytime. There's no minimum commitment required - volunteer as much or as little as your schedule allows.
‚ùì Will I be compensated for my services?
Hand2Hand operates on a volunteer basis. While scribes are not monetarily compensated, you gain valuable experience, contribute to inclusive education, and receive recognition through our platform's rating system. Some institutions may offer volunteer certificates or letters of recommendation for your service.
‚ùì What are my responsibilities as a scribe?
As a scribe, you must: arrive punctually for assigned examinations, write exactly what the student dictates without interpretation or correction, maintain confidentiality of examination content, remain neutral and not influence student responses, follow all examination rules and institutional policies, and report any issues to administrators promptly.
‚ùì Can I decline an assignment?
Yes, you can decline assignments if they don't fit your schedule or expertise. However, we encourage you to update your availability calendar proactively to avoid being assigned during times you're unavailable. Repeated last-minute cancellations may affect your standing on the platform.
‚ùì How do I update my availability?
Log in to your scribe dashboard and navigate to the availability section. You can update your preferred days, time slots, and any blackout dates. We recommend keeping your availability current to ensure you only receive assignments that fit your schedule.

üíª Technical Questions

‚ùì What browsers are supported?
Hand2Hand works best on modern browsers including Google Chrome (version 90+), Mozilla Firefox (version 88+), Microsoft Edge (version 90+), and Safari (version 14+). We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for optimal performance and security.
‚ùì Can I access Hand2Hand on my mobile device?
Yes! Hand2Hand is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. You can register, view dashboards, and manage your account from any device with an internet connection and a web browser.
‚ùì I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
‚ùì Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. Personal information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized administrators. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
‚ùì How do I report a technical issue?
If you encounter any technical problems, please contact us through the Contact Us page. Provide details about the issue including what you were trying to do, any error messages, your browser and device information. Our support team will investigate and respond within 24-48 hours.

üßë‚Äçüíº For Administrators

‚ùì How do I get admin access?
Admin access is granted to authorized personnel from educational institutions. Contact us at support@hand2hand.edu with your institutional details and role to request admin credentials. We'll verify your information and provide access after approval.
‚ùì How do I verify student and scribe registrations?
Log in to the admin dashboard where you'll see pending registrations. Review submitted documents, qualifications, and information. You can approve or reject registrations with a single click. Rejected users receive notification with reasons, and approved users gain full platform access.
‚ùì Can assignments be made manually?
Yes, administrators have the ability to manually assign scribes to students. The platform also offers automated matching suggestions based on criteria like subject expertise, availability, and location. You can choose to use automated suggestions or make manual assignments based on specific requirements.
‚ùì How do I handle conflicts or complaints?
The admin dashboard includes a support ticket system for managing complaints. Review the issue details, contact the involved parties, and mediate a resolution. Document all actions taken for record-keeping and future reference. Serious violations can result in account suspension.
