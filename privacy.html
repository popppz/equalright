Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Hand2Hand is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our scribe support platform.

By using Hand2Hand, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand how your information is handled.

Your Privacy Matters: We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register on Hand2Hand, we collect the following personal information:

User Type Information Collected
Students Name, email, phone number, student ID, institution name, disability details, examination requirements, academic information
Scribes Name, email, phone number, qualifications, educational background, availability, subject expertise, experience details
Administrators Name, email, phone number, institutional affiliation, role designation

2.2 Automatic Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our platform:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the platform
  • Login and activity timestamps
  • Operating system information

2.3 Cookies and Tracking

Hand2Hand uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, maintain sessions, and analyze platform usage. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To match students with appropriate scribes and facilitate examination support
  • Account Management: To create, manage, and authenticate user accounts
  • Communication: To send notifications, updates, and important announcements regarding assignments and platform changes
  • Verification: To verify user credentials, qualifications, and eligibility
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve platform functionality
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address security issues and fraudulent activities
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and institutional requirements
  • Support: To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Within the Platform

Limited information is shared between users as necessary for service delivery:

  • Students receive: Assigned scribe's name, contact information, and qualifications
  • Scribes receive: Student's name, examination details, and special requirements
  • Administrators have access to: All user information for verification and coordination purposes

4.2 Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Educational Institutions: With explicit consent, to verify student eligibility or scribe credentials
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: With trusted technical service providers who assist in platform operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Hand2Hand, users, or the public

4.3 Anonymized Data

We may use and share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify individual users for research, analysis, and reporting purposes.

5. Data Security

Hand2Hand implements robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: User data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security measures are regularly reviewed and updated
  • Password Protection: User passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Activity Monitoring: We monitor for suspicious activities and unauthorized access attempts

Security Note: While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely immune to security breaches. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account remains active
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for more than 2 years may be archived or deleted
  • Examination Records: Records of past examinations and assignments may be retained for institutional and legal compliance purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for dispute resolution

Users can request data deletion by contacting us through the Contact Us page.

7. Your Privacy Rights

As a Hand2Hand user, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint about how your data is handled

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@hand2hand.edu or through our Contact Us page.

8. Children's Privacy

Hand2Hand is designed to support students of all ages, including minors. For users under 18 years of age:

  • We require parental or guardian consent for registration
  • We collect only necessary information for service delivery
  • Special care is taken to protect the privacy of minor users
  • Parents/guardians have the right to access, modify, or delete their child's information

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

9.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and user authentication
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences and settings

9.2 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • We will notify registered users via email
  • Significant changes will be highlighted on the platform

Continued use of Hand2Hand after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
